ABOUT US

You Know It’s Good When It’s Thai Food!

Time for Thai was made out of a desire to bring the captivating and enrapturing effervescence of a classic Thai trattoria to the hub and bub that is Time for Thai's, Kalyani Nagar.
With more than 10 years of cooking experiences in Thai restaurants, our chef is excited to present you and all guests by vision and authentic taste of Thai food. Our caring and committed staff will ensure you a fantastic experience with us. The food shall be focussed on perfecting simple, classic Thai dishes with a nod to the Indian climate and dining sphere, honouring fresh produce and seasonality. We will be working from the foundation up, creating our own pastas and breads, all in house. The desert work similarly; Thai in provenance or variety, they will serve to complement the dishes as they would in their traditional homes.
